## Overview

The Dashboard is your daily nutrition command center, providing an at-a-glance view of today's meal plan and your progress toward nutritional goals. It helps you stay accountable by tracking which meals you've consumed and automatically updating your macronutrient intake.

## What It Does

The Dashboard serves as your daily checkpoint for nutrition management:

- **Daily Meal Overview**: Displays all meals scheduled for today from your weekly nutrition plan
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Shows which meals you've consumed and which are still pending
- **Automatic Macro Calculation**: Updates your nutritional metrics as you mark meals as consumed
- **Goal Progress Monitoring**: Compares your current intake against your personalized targets

## How to Use the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Today's Meals">
    When you open the Dashboard, you'll see all meals planned for today with their nutritional information.
    
    Each meal card displays:
    - Recipe name and image
    - Calories, protein, carbs, and fat content
    - Consumption status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark Meals as Consumed">
    After eating a meal, click the **"Mark as consumed"** button on that meal card.
    
    The button will change to show a checkmark and "Consumed" status.
    
    <Note>
      Your macronutrient totals update instantly when you mark a meal as consumed.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Your Progress">
    Check the macronutrient cards at the top of the Dashboard to see:
    - How many calories you've consumed vs. your daily goal
    - Your protein, carb, and fat intake progress
    - Visual progress bars for each macro
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust If Needed">
    If you accidentally mark a meal as consumed, click the "Consumed" button again to toggle it back to pending status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## UI Components

### Macronutrient Cards

The top section displays four cards showing your daily nutritional targets:

- **Calories**: Total energy intake in kcal
- **Proteins**: Muscle-building macros in grams
- **Carbohydrates**: Energy source in grams
- **Fats**: Essential fats in grams

Each card shows:
- Current intake (from consumed meals)
- Daily goal (based on your profile)
- Progress indicator

### Meal Cards

Each meal is presented in an easy-to-read card format:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pending Meal">
    - Recipe image thumbnail
    - Recipe name
    - Nutritional breakdown
    - "Mark as consumed" button (gray)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Consumed Meal">
    - Same information as pending
    - "Consumed" button (highlighted with checkmark)
    - Indicates meal has been tracked
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Morning Routine Check-In">
    Start your day by reviewing the Dashboard to see what meals are planned. This helps you mentally prepare and ensures you have ingredients ready.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Post-Meal Tracking">
    After each meal, open the Dashboard and mark it as consumed. This builds accountability and helps you stay aware of your nutritional intake throughout the day.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Mid-Day Progress Review">
    Check in during lunch or afternoon to see how much of your daily nutrition goals you've achieved and what's remaining.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="End-of-Day Reflection">
    Review your Dashboard at night to see if you met your goals. This data helps you understand your eating patterns over time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Adjusting Meal Consumption">
    If you skip a meal or eat something different, you can leave it unmarked or toggle the status to accurately reflect your actual intake.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Dashboard States

<Warning>
  **No Active Plan**: If you haven't generated a nutrition plan yet, the Dashboard will display a prompt to create one through the AI Chat feature.
</Warning>

<Info>
  **Loading State**: When first accessing the Dashboard, you'll see a loading indicator while your plan data is retrieved.
</Info>

<Check>
  **Active Plan**: Once you have an active weekly plan, the Dashboard automatically shows today's meals and updates in real-time.
</Check>

## Tips for Best Results

<Tip>
  **Track Consistently**: Mark meals as consumed as soon as you finish eating for the most accurate tracking.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Review Daily**: Make it a habit to check your Dashboard at least twice a day - morning and evening.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use Visual Cues**: The color-coded progress bars help you quickly identify which macros you're on track with.
</Tip>
